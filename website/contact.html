Try FormBridge Live

This form connects to our real serverless API. Submit a message and we'll store it in DynamoDB and send an email.

How This Works

  1. 1. Fill out the form below with your message.
  2. 2. Click "Send" to submit to our /submit endpoint.
  3. 3. Your message is validated, rate-limited, and stored in DynamoDB.
  4. 4. An email is sent via AWS SES and the submission ID is returned.
  5. 5. You see a success toast with the submission ID.
