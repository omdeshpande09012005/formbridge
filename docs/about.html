Frequently Asked Questions

Is FormBridge really free?

Yes! Our free tier includes up to 100 submissions per month. Premium plans start at $9/month for more submissions and advanced features like webhooks and custom templates.

How secure is FormBridge?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest. We support HMAC-SHA256 signing for request verification, and all infrastructure is on AWS with industry-leading compliance certifications.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely! You can export all your submissions as CSV or JSON from the analytics dashboard anytime. Your data is always yours.

What about API rate limits?

Free tier has generous limits (1,000 requests per day). Premium tiers offer higher limits with auto-scaling support for any traffic level. Contact us for enterprise pricing.

Do you have an SLA?

Yes! We guarantee 99.99% uptime with automatic failover and monitoring. If we fall short, you get service credits.
